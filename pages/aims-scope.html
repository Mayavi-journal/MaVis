Aims & Scope

MaVis publishes high-quality research across intelligent systems and applications. Topics include (but are not limited to):

  • AI & Machine Learning
  • Intelligent Systems and Smart Applications
  • Learning Analytics and Educational Technology
  • Decision-support systems and applied data science
  • Ethical, human-centered intelligent systems

MaVis publishes regular issues, and selected peer-reviewed conference papers as special issues. The journal is international and interdisciplinary.
