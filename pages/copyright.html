Copyright and Open Access Policy

MaVis is an open access journal. All articles published in the journal are freely accessible to readers without subscription barriers.

Copyright

Authors retain copyright of their published work. By submitting to MaVis, authors grant the journal the right to publish and distribute the article.

Licensing

Articles are published under a Creative Commons license, allowing reuse with proper attribution to the original authors and source.

Archiving

MaVis permits authors to archive preprints and published versions of their articles in institutional or personal repositories.
