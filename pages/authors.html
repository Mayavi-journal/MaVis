Information for Authors

Article Types

  • Original Research Articles
  • Review Articles
  • Short Communications
  • Case Studies
  • Selected Peer-Reviewed Conference Papers (Special Issues)

Manuscript Preparation

  • Language: English
  • Length: 6,000–8,000 words (research articles)
  • Format: PDF for initial submission
  • References: IEEE or APA style

Submission Procedure

Authors should submit manuscripts via email to: mavis.journal@protonmail.com

Each submission must include:

  • Manuscript file
  • Cover letter
  • Declaration of originality

Publication Charges

MaVis does not charge any submission or publication fees at the current stage.


Copyright and Licensing

Authors retain copyright of their work. Articles are published under an open access license that permits unrestricted use, distribution, and reproduction, provided the original work is properly cited.
